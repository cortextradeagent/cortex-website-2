Frequently asked questions

Can I switch vaults anytime?

Yes, you can move between vaults at any time with no lock-up period. Withdrawals are processed within the same block when possible.

How is the performance fee calculated?

Performance fees are only charged on profits above your high-water mark. If your vault doesn't generate profit, you pay nothing.

What tokens can I deposit?

Currently we accept SOL, USDC, and USDT. The vault automatically converts and allocates based on optimal strategy positioning.

Are the smart contracts audited?

Yes, all contracts are audited by OtterSec and Neodyme. The vaults are non-custodial—you retain full control of your assets at all times.
